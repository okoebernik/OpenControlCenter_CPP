\# OpenControlCenter



\## Vision



OpenControlCenter is not a single application.



OpenControlCenter is a platform.



One Platform. Unlimited Systems.



OCC brings information from different systems together into one coherent interface.



It should be able to visualize, monitor and control systems such as:



\- smart home

\- MQTT devices

\- aquarium systems

\- Victron energy systems

\- Synology and Proxmox

\- weather stations

\- fire department systems

\- cameras

\- industrial devices

\- future extensions



\## Philosophy



Architecture before implementation.



Readability before cleverness.



Composition before inheritance.



Loose coupling before convenience.



Documentation before memory.



Refactoring is part of development.



Every bug teaches us something.



Every feature should make the platform better.
